NATURE'S TREASURE

Panda: Gentle Giants Of China

Pandas are gentle, bamboo-eating bears found mainly in China. They are famous for their black-and-white fur and playful nature, and they symbolize wildlife conservation worldwide.

Diet: Though classified as carnivores, pandas eat 99% bamboo. They may occasionally eat small animals or fish.

WHY PANDAS MATTER

- Cultural symbol: Pandas are considered a national treasure in China.
- Conservation icon: They represent global efforts to protect endangered species.
- Ecological role: By eating and spreading bamboo seeds, pandas help maintain forest ecosystems.
